If you want to add the selected links to default video with type 1 or 2 only, or do not need default video at all, replace in the bookmarklet "keep=3" to "keep=1" or "keep=2", or "keep=0".
If you want to add selected video only to the left (right) player, replace in the bookmarklet text "type=3" to "type=1" ( "type=2").

To do it click on the bookmarklet with right button of mouse , then "Edit...":
